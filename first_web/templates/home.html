MOTIVATION CORNER

Subedar Joginder Singh was born on 26th September 1921 in Mahakalan village in Moga district of Punjab. Son of Sri Sher Singh Saini and Bibi Krishnan Kaur, Sub Joginder Singh completed his primary education in his village but could not continue his studies further as his parents could not afford it. He joined the British Army on 28 Sep 1936 and was recruited into 1 Sikh of the Sikh Regiment.
He participated in World War II on Burma front and then also took part in Indo-Pak war of 1947-48 in Srinagar and Indo-China War which took place in 1962.
